Registration Instructions

The registration link can be found below. Before jumping in, please take into account the following instructions

  • The registration form works as a shopping cart, where you should add one registration "item" (student or regular) unless the committee has waived your registration fee.
  • There will be commemorative T-shirts for the 20th anniversary of the workshop available for purchase. You can add as many as like. The color/size will be requested at a later date using the e-mail you provide.
  • T-shirts can only be ordered until April 15th, 2023
  • If you would like to attend the workshop dinner, do not forget to add the corresponding item.
  • The form requires a US number, but if you don't have one, just enter (111) 111-1111.
  • After registration, you should see a confirmation webpage and also receive an e-mail.
