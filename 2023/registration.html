Registration Instructions

The registration link can be found below. Before jumping in, please take into account the following instructions

  • The registration form works as a shopping cart, where you should add one registration "item" (student or regular) unless the committee has waived your registration fee.
  • There will be commemorative T-shirts for the 20th anniversary of the workshop available for purchase. You can purchase as many as like (there is no T-shirt included with the registration).
  • Please use the comment section to indicate your T-shirt letter size (for example, XS, S, M, etc.).
  • T-shirts can only be ordered until April 15th, 2023
  • If you would like to attend the workshop dinner, do not forget to add the corresponding item. The workshop dinner will be held on May 22nd (Monday).
  • USC dorm rooms are available for booking through the registration form (60 USD per night per person, limited availability). These are shared rooms (2 people per room). Each $60 item corresponds to one night. Please indicate your dates in the comment section.
  • The form requires a US number, but if you don't have one, just enter (111) 111-1111.
  • After registration, you should see a confirmation webpage and also receive an e-mail.
