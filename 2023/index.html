Mixed Integer Programming Workshop 2023

May 22-25, 2023

University of Southern California

About

The Mixed Integer Programming (MIP) Workshop is a single-track workshop highlighting the latest trends in integer programming and discrete optimization, with speakers chosen by invitation.

The 2023 edition of the workshop will be the twentieth in the MIP series.

Computational competition

The 2023 MIP Workshop will host a competition for research teams to showcase their practical, computational prowess. More details on the specifics of the competition to come!
