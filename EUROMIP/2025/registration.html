Prices

The registration for the workshop includes access to all presentations, as well as coffee breaks and lunches on all days. Moreover, we could arrange free accommodation for 50 student participants. If you are a student and interested in free accommodation, you can sign up for this option during the registration. The available rooms will be assigned on a first come first serve basis, i.e., if more than 50 students are interested in free accommodation, only the 50 first can be served. All student rooms are single-bed rooms (9 m2) with a private bathroom; exemplary pictures can be found via this link (in French).

Participants who are interested in attending the conference dinner can register for the dinner via the registration form (see below for cost). The dinner will take place on July 2.

Registration fees (incl. taxes)

  • students: €66
