Registration

Registration for MIP 2025 is open at https://z.umn.edu/mip2025_registration. All registrations include access to the summer school (Monday, June 2, 2025) and regular workshop talks (June 3-6, 2025), as well as a ticket to the poster session / welcome reception on Tuesday, June 3, 2025. There is an additional option for the workshop dinner at a well-regarded local venue, The Riverside, for Wednesday, June 4, 2025.
