Conference Venue

The workshop will be held at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities.

  • Summer school, June 2, 2025: Keller Hall 3-180, located at 200 Union Street SE, Minneapolis, MN 55455.
  • Workshop, June 3-6, 2025: Keller Hall 3-180, located at 200 Union Street SE, Minneapolis, MN 55455.
  • Poster session and reception, June 3, 2025: Campus Club, located on the 4th floor of Coffman Memorial Union at 300 Washington Ave SE, Minneapolis, MN 55455.

Welcoming Statement

The MIP 2025 organizing committee and the local organizing committee are committed to making the workshop a welcoming environment for all members of the MIP community.

Lodging

For both students and nonstudents, dorm rooms are available for reservation at $30 per night. The rooms are private, with shared bathrooms. If you would like this option, please check the relevant box when registering.
