MIP 2026 Workshop Schedule

The workshop program is available, and below is the schedule (subject to change) for the 2026 Mixed Integer Programming Workshop. (Click the picture for a pdf version of the full week schedule.) The location of the workshop is University of Connecticut, Stamford Campus, with all workshop talks in DWTN 109 (A1) and all tea/coffee breaks outside.

Generally, the daily schedule will be (with deviations for special events): registration at 9:15am, first invited talk at 9:30am, a first series of flash talks at 10:05, a coffee break at 10:30, followed by the second and third invited talks at 11:00 before the lunch break. At 2:05pm we continue with two invited talks, a coffee break at 3:10pm, and the last series of invited talks at 3:40pm. On Monday we will have poster teasers around the afternoon coffee break, and the poster session in the evening. On Tuesday the outcomes of the computation competition are presented at 4:50, after which a group photo will be taken, which is then celebrated at the conference dinner at 6:00pm. On Wednesday the MIPS Business Meeting takes place at 4:15pm. The workshop concludes on Thursday around 12:00pm.
