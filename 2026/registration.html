Registration

Registration for MIP 2026 is open at this website at University of Connecticut. All registrations include access to the workshop talks (May 18-21, 2026), as well as a ticket to the poster session / welcome reception on Monday, May 18, 2026. There is an additional option for the workshop dinner, for Tuesday, May 19, 2026.

Contact david.bergman@uconn.edu for inquiries about payment.
