Mixed Integer Programming Workshop 2022

feat. DANniversary

May 23-26, 2022

DIMACS, Rutgers University

About

The Mixed Integer Programming (MIP) Workshop is a single-track workshop highlighting the latest trends in integer programming and discrete optimization, with speakers chosen by invitation.

The MIP 2022 edition of the workshop will be the nineteenth in the MIP series, and it will be opened by DANniversary, a special conference in celebration of Daniel Bienstock's 60th birthday.

Computational competition

This year, we will also host a computational competition. All relevant information can be found here.

Poster session

We will host a student poster session, which includes a best poster award. Students are invited to submit an abstract of their work (pdf file, up to 500 words). The deadline for the abstract submission is March, 10th. The program committee will select the poster competition finalists and presenters. Travel support will be offered to selected students, both US-based and non-US-based.
