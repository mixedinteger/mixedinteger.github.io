About

MIP South America will be held in Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez, Viña del Mar Campus.

The Viña del Mar campus is located in Los Altos del Sporting Club. Characterized by its impressive view of the sea, it is made up of six buildings, where all the activities of students and teachers take place.

Getting there

To get to the UAI Viña del Mar Campus, the workshop will have shuttle buses for participants. The shuttle buses will leave in the mornings towards the UAI, and will return at the end of the day to the same place. The specific departure point will be communicated in due course.
