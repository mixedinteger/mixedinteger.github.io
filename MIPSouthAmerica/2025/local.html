About

MIP South America will be held in Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez, Viña del Mar Campus.

The Viña del Mar campus is located in Los Altos del Sporting Club. Characterized by its impressive view of the sea, it is made up of six buildings, where all the activities of students and teachers take place.

Getting there

To get to the UAI Viña del Mar Campus from the city center, the workshop will have shuttle buses for participants. The shuttle buses will leave in the mornings towards the UAI, and will return at the end of the day to the same place. The specific departure point will be communicated in due course, but it will be on the green area of the map below.

If you do not take the daily shuttle, the campus can be reached in approximately 10 minutes by car or taxi/Uber from the green area.

Lodging


We recommend booking accommodations within the green area shown on the map above. This neighborhood offers a wide range of lodging options and is within walking distance of restaurants, the beach, and the departure point for the daily workshop shuttle (final location to be confirmed).

Hotel Special Rates
