Registration

Registration for MIP South America 2025 is open HERE. Registrations include access to all regular workshop talks (December 9-12, 2025), and to the poster session / welcome reception on Tuesday, December 9, 2025. There is an additional option for the workshop dinner, for Wednesday, December 10, 2025.

Prices

Early registration fees (until September 30 October 17, 2025)

  • students: $60.000 CLP (or 70 USD)
  • non-students: $120.000 CLP (or 130 USD)

Standard registration fees (after September 30 October 17, 2025)

  • students: $80.000 CLP (or 90 USD)
  • non-students: $150.000 CLP (or 160 USD)

Dinner: 70.000 CLP (or 80 USD)

Heads up about USD payments

Some participants have reported issues with the USD payment with credit card. We are trying to solve this, but you can still register by selecting CLP (Chilean pesos) and letting your card do the currency conversion. This has worked without issues. You can still proceed to the registration page now. If you try USD and it fails, try switching to CLP.
