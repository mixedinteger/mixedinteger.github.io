COMIPS Elections

Mixed-Integer Programming Society

November 18 - December 9, 2022

About

COMIPS is the governing board of the Mixed-Integer Programming Society, a recently created section of the Mathematical Optimization Society. Its main duties are: ensuring the continuity of the MIP Workshop, approving the new MIP organizing committee and auditing its financial reports.

The COMIPS consists of three elected officials, plus the chairs of the last two MIP organizing committees, which in this case correspond to:

  • Yuri Faenza (Columbia University)
  • Yuan Zhou (University of Kentucky)
  • Carla Michini (University of Wisconsin-Madison)

You can find the bylaws here

Below you can find the list of candidates (in alphabetical order) for the first COMIPS election. The voting portal is Election Runner; every eligible voter will receive credentials during Dec. 18 and can vote for up to three candidates.
