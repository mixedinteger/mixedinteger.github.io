COMIPS Elections

Mixed-Integer Programming Society

January - March, 2025

About

COMIPS is the governing board of the Mixed-Integer Programming Society, a section of the Mathematical Optimization Society. Its main duties are: ensuring the continuity of the MIP Workshop, approving the new MIP organizing committee and auditing its financial reports.

The COMIPS consists of three elected officials, plus the chairs of the last two MIP organizing committees, which in this case correspond to:

  • Aleksandr Kazachkov (University of Florida)
  • Joseph Paat (University of British Columbia)

You can find the bylaws here

Below you can find the list of candidates (in alphabetical order by last name) for the COMIPS election. The voting portal is Election Runner; every eligible voter will receive credentials on Feb. 26 and can vote for up to three candidates.
